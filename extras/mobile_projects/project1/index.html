Power 96.1

Power 96.1 is Atlanta's top Hip Hop/Rap radio station. It was formed in 1960 and boasts a number of on-air celebrities such as Ryan Seacrest and Elvis Duran. It's slogan is "Atlanta's New Hit Music Station."

Power Athens 100.1

Power Athens 100.1 is a Top 40 Hits station based in Watkinsville. It mainly serves the Athens' Metro area. It began broadcasting in 1978. They play a variety of genres, ranging from Hip-Hop, Soft rock, Folk, and Indie.

NPR 90.1

National Public Radio (NPR) is a publicly funded non-profit national syndicator to over 900 public radio stations all across the United States. It's first broadcast date was in 1971. The broadcast signal in Georgia is 90.1. There are two main news broadcasts: Morning Edition, a morning news broadcast and All Things Considered, an afternoon broadcast. These are two of the most popular radio programs in the nation.

Album 88.5

88.5 FM WRAS is Georgia State's Radio station. It has been in operation since 1971 and it has the highest power of any student-staffed radio station in the world (100k watts ERP). It is based in Atlanta and it's on-air staff are all GSU students. Album 88.5 plays a diverse array of music, many of which are underground and indie artists.

90.5 WUOG

90.5 WUOG is the University of Georgia's radio station. It first began broadcasting in 1972 and is based in Athens. The transmitter for the station is on top of Brumby hall, a freshman all-girls dorm. R.E.M. a popular, renown band was first broadcasted on WUOG back in 1980. The station is maintained and run by a staff of student volunteers. WUOG offers regular rotation programming and also specialty shows. WUOG's music philosophy strives to incide artists whose music is rarely heard elsewhere. WUOG also offers talk and sports shows throughout the week.

91.1 WREK

91.1 WREK is The Georgia Institute of Technology's student run radio station. It began broadcasting in 1968. It is based in Atlanta and is named after "Rambling Wreck" which is a mascot of Georgia Tech. WREK's slogan is "quality diverse radio" as it's programming is very diverse ranging from heavy metal to world, hiphop to blues, classical and jazz to industrial and noise.

The River 97.1

97.1 The River is a classic rock hits radio station based in Gainsville that plays pop and rock hits from the 1960's-1980's. The station was created in 1972 but it was not until 2006 when it became "The River."

 

Rock 100.7

Rock 100.7 WPPP is an Athens' Based alternative rock station. Its broadcast within a 15 mile radius near the University of Georgia's Campus. WPPP's programming also includes "news reports and progressive political commentary produced free of corporate interests and government influence."

100.5 FM WNNX

Rock 100.5 WNNX is a hard rock radio station based in College Park. It first broadcasted in 2001 and mainly serves the Atlanta metropolitan area. The main transmitter is located atop the Westin Peachtree Plaza Hotel. One of the well known programming from Rock 100.5 is The Regular Guys morning show.

The Bull 94.9

94.9 The Bull is a country rock station based in northeast Atlanta near Druid hills. The station began broadcasting in 1962. Its broadcast area is mainly in the metro-Atlanta area.

The Fish 104.7

104.7 The Fish is a Christian music radio station based in Athens Georgia. The station is a hybrid of christian pop and clean secular music with a mainstream feel. The broadcast area is mainly in the metro-Atlanta area.

About Dawgs Radio

Dawgs Radio was created in order to have a directory of radio stations that broadcast cover the Athens area. As a student of the Univeristy of Georgia, I am constantly influenced by music daily, hearing it around campus, downtown, in class, and on my iPod and computer. Dawgs radio sets to promote diversity of music as there are many different genres being broadcasted over the airwaves.
